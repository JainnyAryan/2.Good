Amend

A place where you can post on the handles of mainstream brands and their products. You can post about series of problems that are being faced by you and your feedbacks and can discuss it all with other users. It just doesnt stop there, we send all of the information collected by you to the developer so that it helps in making the product better for you.

Append

Some times the tools you use are just not enough or up to mark, we bring out "Append" to you, where in you can request you features to your respective developers directly.

Analytics

Knowing whether the problems faced by you is being faced by other gives the user a sense of relief. At Analytics we give a graphical representation of the problems that are related to your problems so that its easier for you to analyse.

PROBLEMS FACED BY USERS

Irrelevent solutions provided by the Moderators

Major companies/brands collect customer feedback via forms and mails. But generally, the company's response to it isn't quick or helpful enough. The solutions given by them either are not relevant or are just traditional irrelevant solutions.

User's requests are left unaddressed

Some users expect from the developers some new desired features. But the developers are unaware of this demand of users. A few users may request a feature, but their requests are left unaddressed.

Unawareness among the users and lack of knowledge about the product

Many users are unaware of the existing features or solutions within the product. Looking for the solution, that user hops from page to page throughout the web, and then returns empty-handed. Community platforms like Reddit, Quora, etc. have users discussing the solutions, but there is an enormous bunch of information available which isn't relevant enough to the user.
